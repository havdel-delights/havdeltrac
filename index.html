FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the price? +
The price includes the GPS tracker device, professional installation by our technician, and access to the tracking dashboard. Contact us for a full breakdown of monthly fees.
Do I need to download an app? +
Yes. You can access the tracking platform from any phone, tablet, or computer using a browser — or via the mobile app. No dedicated installation required.
How long is route history kept? +
Route history is stored and fully accessible for playback. Contact us for details on your specific plan.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment? +
We offer flexible plans with no long-term contracts required. Contact us to discuss the arrangement that works best for you.
What vehicles can be tracked? +
We can install trackers on virtually any vehicle — cars, trucks, vans, motorbikes, buses, and more. Contact us to discuss your specific vehicle type.
How do I get support if something goes wrong? +
You can reach us via WhatsApp, phone, or email. Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9am–5pm, and we aim to respond as quickly as possible.

Who We Are

Exceptional Tracking Solutions, Built on Excellence

Havdeltrac is a GPS car tracking company based in Kampala, dedicated to delivering exceptional tracking solutions to vehicle owners throughout Uganda and the surrounding regions.

We specialize in the installation and monitoring of high-quality GPS tracking systems compatible with all vehicle brands and models. Our team consists of highly trained professionals equipped with cutting-edge technology and the expertise necessary to provide superior service.

Our commitment to excellence underpins our pursuit of financial success through unwavering service quality and integrity.
